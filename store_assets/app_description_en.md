## Full Description

Towa is a shift schedule management app designed for shift workers.

Simply drag across the calendar to set up your shift cycle — then see today's shift at a glance on the app and your home screen widget.

**Key Features**

• Drag-to-paint shift cycle setup
  Drag your finger across the calendar dates to assign shift types (day/night/off) in one smooth motion.

• Per-day override
  Need to swap a single day? Tap any date in the calendar to change just that day without affecting your cycle.

• Home screen widget
  See today's shift name and hours right on your home screen — no need to open the app.
  iOS: Small / Medium / Large / Lock Screen widgets
  Android: 2×1 / 2×2 / 4×2 widgets

• Pre-shift notifications
  Get notified minutes before your shift starts so you're always prepared.

• Custom shift types
  Beyond the defaults (Day / Night / Off), add your own shift types with custom names, hours, and colors.

• Multiple schedules
  Manage multiple shift patterns — for different jobs or family members — and switch between them easily.

**Perfect for**
- Hospital, factory, retail, and service industry workers
- Anyone with repeating irregular shift patterns
- People who want instant access to today's schedule without unlocking their phone
