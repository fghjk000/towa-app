# Privacy Policy

**App**: Towa
**Last updated**: February 26, 2026

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Towa does not collect any personal information.

All data you enter in the app (shift schedules, shift types, notification settings, etc.) is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to any external server.

---

## 2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

- **App data**: Local storage on device (Hive database)
- **Widget data**: On-device shared storage (iOS: App Group, Android: SharedPreferences)

No data is sent to servers, cloud services, or any third party.

---

## 3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Notifications | Send reminders before your shift starts |
| Exact Alarm (Android) | Deliver notifications at the precise scheduled time |

These permissions are only needed for the respective features. Denying them will not affect core app functionality (schedule viewing, widget).

---

## 4. Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect personal information, there is nothing to share with third parties.

---

## 5. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Towa does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will notify you through an app update.
